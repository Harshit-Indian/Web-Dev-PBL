System Features

Feature 1: Digital Token Generation

Users can get a token number in different ways:

  • Using mobile app
  • Using website
  • Using kiosk machine at the location
  • Taking help from staff

The token has a unique number like A-101, B-205 etc. Users also get a QR code that they can show at the counter.

Feature 2: Live Queue Tracking

Users can see their position in the queue in real-time. They can check:

  • Their token number
  • Current position in queue
  • Which token is being served now
  • How many people are ahead of them

This information is shown on mobile app, website, and display screens at the location.

Feature 3: Wait Time Prediction

The system can tell users approximately how long they have to wait. For example:

  • Your estimated wait time: 25 minutes
  • Your turn will come around: 2:30 PM

The system calculates this based on how fast the queue is moving and how many people are ahead.

Feature 4: SMS and App Notifications

Users get notifications on their phone:

  • When they get the token
  • When they are 10 people away from their turn
  • When they are 5 people away from their turn
  • When it's their turn

Notifications are sent through SMS (text message) and mobile app.

Feature 5: Physical and Digital Tokens Both

Our system works for everyone:

  • Digital Token: For people who use smartphones
  • Physical Token: Printed paper token for people who don't use smartphones

Both types of tokens work in the same queue. Nobody is left out.

Feature 6: Easy for Elderly and Disabled People

The system is designed to be easy for everyone:

  • Big text and buttons for people with weak eyesight
  • Voice announcements for people who can't see well
  • Simple interface with less buttons
  • Staff available to help
  • Priority queue for elderly and disabled people
  • Available in multiple languages

Feature 7: Low Cost System

This system doesn't need expensive equipment:

  • Works with basic computers
  • Can use simple TV screens for display
  • Internet connection needed (which most places already have)
  • Optional: Kiosk machines for printing tokens

Even small clinics and offices can afford this system.
