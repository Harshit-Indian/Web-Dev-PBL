About First In Line

The Problem We Are Solving

Long queues are a big problem in many places like hospitals, government offices, banks, and colleges. People have to stand for hours without knowing when their turn will come.

Main Problems:

  • Time Wastage: People waste 2-4 hours just standing in lines
  • No Information: Nobody knows how long they have to wait
  • Physical Difficulty: Standing for long time is hard for elderly people and pregnant women
  • Frustration: People get angry and stressed because of uncertainty
  • Crowding: Too many people in small waiting areas

Our Design Thinking Process

We used the Design Thinking method to create this solution. Design Thinking has 5 steps:

Step What We Did
1. Empathize We talked to people who stand in queues. We asked them about their problems and feelings.
2. Define We wrote down the main problem: People waste time and get frustrated in queues because they don't know when their turn will come.
3. Ideate We thought of many solutions like digital tokens, mobile apps, SMS notifications, and display boards.
4. Prototype We made a simple design of our system and showed it to people.
5. Test We asked people to try our design and give feedback. We made improvements based on their suggestions.

Who Will Use This System?

1. Hospital Patients

People who visit hospitals and clinics for treatment and checkups.

2. Government Office Visitors

Citizens who need to get documents, certificates, and licenses from government offices.

3. College Students

Students who need to submit forms, get certificates, and meet teachers.

4. Bank Customers

People who go to banks for money transactions and other services.

5. Elderly People

Senior citizens who find it difficult to stand in long queues.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make waiting in queues easier and less frustrating for everyone. We want to save people's time and reduce stress.

This is a college project for our Design Thinking course. We hope that this system can be used in real places to help people.
