Welcome to First In Line

A Queue Management System for Hospitals, Banks, Government Offices and More

What is First In Line?

First In Line is a token-based queue management system that helps reduce waiting time in hospitals, government offices, colleges, banks, and service centers. Instead of standing in long lines, people can get a token and wait comfortably.

This is a Design Thinking project created to solve the problem of long queues and waiting time frustration.

The Problem

Every day, people waste hours standing in long queues at various places. They don't know how long they have to wait. This causes frustration and wastes valuable time.

Our Solution

First In Line gives people a token number. They can track their position in the queue and get notifications when their turn is coming. No need to stand in line!

Main Features

Digital Tokens Get tokens on mobile or kiosk
Live Tracking See your queue position in real-time
Time Prediction Know approximately when your turn will come
Notifications Get SMS and app notifications

Benefits

  • Save time - no need to stand in line
  • Less frustration and stress
  • Know your waiting time
  • Easy to use for everyone
  • Works for elderly and disabled people too
