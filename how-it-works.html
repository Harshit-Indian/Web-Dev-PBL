How It Works

First In Line works in 5 simple steps. Anyone can use it easily.

Step 1: Get Your Token

First, you need to get a token. You can get it in these ways:

Option A: Mobile App or Website

  • Open the First In Line app on your phone
  • OR visit the website on your phone or computer
  • Select the service you need (like "General Checkup" or "Account Opening")
  • Click on "Get Token" button
  • You will get a token number like A-145

Option B: Kiosk Machine

  • Go to the kiosk machine at the location
  • Touch the screen
  • Select your service type
  • Press the "Print Token" button
  • Take the printed token paper

Option C: Ask Staff

  • Go to the help desk
  • Tell them what service you need
  • They will give you a token

Step 2: Token Confirmation

After getting your token, you will see this information:

  • Token Number: A-145
  • Service Type: General Consultation
  • Your Position: 23 (means 23 people are ahead of you)
  • Estimated Wait Time: Approximately 35 minutes
  • Current Token Being Served: A-122

You will also get this information on SMS if you provided your phone number.

Step 3: Track Your Position

Now you don't have to stand in line. You can:

  • Sit in the waiting area
  • Go to nearby cafe or shop
  • Do other work
  • Relax

How to Check Your Position:

  • Mobile App: Open the app to see your current position
  • Website: Enter your token number on website to check
  • Display Screen: Look at the big TV screens showing token numbers
  • SMS: You will get text messages with updates

The system updates your position every time someone's turn is completed.

Step 4: Get Notifications

The system will send you notifications at important times:

When Notification Message
15 minutes before your turn "Your turn is coming soon. Please start coming to the location."
5 people before you "Only 5 people ahead of you. Please be ready."
Your turn "Token A-145, please come to Counter 3 now."

You will hear these announcements on the speakers also.

Step 5: Go to Counter and Get Service

When you get the notification that it's your turn:

  1. Go to the counter number shown in the notification (like Counter 3)
  2. Show your token to the staff person
    • If you have digital token: Show the QR code on your phone
    • If you have paper token: Give the paper to staff
  3. Staff will scan or check your token
  4. You will get your service
  5. Done! Your work is completed
